# CASIA-SURF

- **Year:** 2019
- **Subjects:** 1000
- **Live/Spoof:** 3000/18000 (Video)
- **Setup:** VIS, Depth, NIR; Background removed; Randomly cut eyes, nose or mouth areas
- **Attack Types:** Print(flat, wrapped, cut)
- **Link:** [Paper](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content_CVPR_2019/papers/Zhang_A_Dataset_and_Benchmark_for_Large-Scale_Multi-Modal_Face_Anti-Spoofing_CVPR_2019_paper.pdf)

## Description
A Large-Scale Multi-Modal Face Anti-Spoofing Dataset.
