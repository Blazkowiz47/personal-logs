# CelebA-Spoof

- **Year:** 2020
- **Subjects:** 10,177
- **Live/Spoof:** 156,384 / 469,153 (Image)
- **Setup:** 4 illumination conditions; indoor & outdoor; rich annotations
- **Attack Types:** Print(flat, wrapped), Replay(monitor tablet, phone), Mask(paper)
- **Link:** [Paper](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-030-58610-2_5)

## Description
A large-scale face anti-spoofing dataset with rich annotations.
