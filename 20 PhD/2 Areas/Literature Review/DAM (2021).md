## Quick Summary
**Method:** DAM (Dataset and Benchmark)
- **Dataset:** DAM (Dataset for Anti-spoofing in Multi-modal surveillance)
- **Modalities:** RGB, Depth, Infrared (IR)
- **Scenario:** Surveillance (low resolution, pose variations, complex lighting)

## Problem Statement
Existing FAS datasets are mostly controlled/cooperative. Surveillance scenarios (unconstrained) are under-represented.

## Key Contributions
1.  **DAM Dataset:** Large-scale multi-modal dataset for surveillance FAS.
2.  **Benchmark:** Evaluates state-of-the-art methods on this challenging data.
